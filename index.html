Leonardo DiCaprio was born on November 11, 1974, in Los Angeles, California. Leonardo DiCaprio is an American actor known for his good looks and edgy, unconventional roles. DiCaprio started out in television in the early 1990s. This Boy’s Life (1993) with Robert De Niro was his first major film role.


Prior to “The Great Gatsby”, he starred in “Django Unchained, ” where he received a Golden Globe nomination for his work. As the title role in “J.
